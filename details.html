Tentang Wisata

Nusa Penida is an island southeast of indonesia's island Bali and a district of Klungkung Regency that includes the neighbouring samll island of Nusa Lembongan. The Badung Strait separates the island and Bali. The interior of Nusa Penida is hilly with a maximum altitude of 524 meters. It is drier that the nearby island of Bali.
